About our Business


Welcome to Portable Solutions. We are a small WA family run business providing rental services for portable power, solar, refrigeration, lighting and more.

Unlike most rental services we don't require you to visit a store to place orders and fill out paper when you can do it at home. We don't charge an upfront bond to all our customers when only a very small number of people make a bond necessary.

We talk about more than just your equipment, we talk about where you're going and explore exciting things at your destination. This also gives us more exciting things to talk about with other customers, contributing to the community of like-minded travellers and sharing experiences that give everyone a better getaway.

About our Origin


This business was born from our love of exploring, travelling and camping off grid and finding those places that make you feel proud and grateful to be a West Australian.

Our journey has led us to finding the most cost effective, simple to use and most convenient ways to maintaining basic needs like power when you need it, fresh food, cold drinks and keeping yourself clean while on the road.

Our Managing Director Benjamin Beitz is a qualified Automotive Electrician, with the knowledge of electrical systems and first-hand experience of exploring WA it was a logical choice to combine these two things into a practical service so others can benefit from the lessons learned along the way.

At Portable Solutions we love to hear about where your headed and want to find the best solution for you that is simple, hassle free and most of all worth it!

Thank you for visiting our website and we hope to hear from you soon!
