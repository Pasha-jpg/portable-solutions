Q. How loud are the Portable Power Stations?

A. The fans on the power stations operate at roughly 20db, about the same as a whisper from 1.5m away or wind blowing over leaves.

Q. What safety features do the power stations have built in?

A. We use Itechworld Portable Power Stations because of their compliant safety features and product reliability.

They have an overload protection button on the side of their 2000w Portable Power Station, built in MPPT solar regulator with over charge protection, power cut off in high temperature environments, digital icons on the screen to indicate faults or extreme conditions, charge and discharge regulation to power devices and charge simultaneously and UPS pass through technology that allows the battery to take over with a 10ms delay when connected to both an appliance and to the grid.

Q. Do you charge a bond?

The details of how we use this is fully explained in our customer hire agreement.
